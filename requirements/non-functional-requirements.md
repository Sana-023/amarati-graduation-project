# Non- Functional Requirements

## 1. Performance
- 1.1 The system shall handle up to 100 concurrent users without noticeable delay.


## 2. Usability
- 2.1 The interface shall be intuitive and accessible for users with different technical backgrounds.
- 2.2 The system shall follow basic accessibility guidelines (readable fonts, clear contrast).


## 3. Availability
- 3.1 The system shall maintain a minimum uptime of 99% .


## 4. Privacy
- 4.1 Access to personal and unit-related data shall be role-based, ensuring users only view information relevant to their role.
- 4.2 The system shall comply with local data protection laws (Saudi Personal Data Protection Law).


## 5. Scalability
- 5.1 The system architecture shall support scaling to accommodate additional buildings, users, and maintenance providers without major redesign.
